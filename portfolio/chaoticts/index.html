Generative latent chaotic timeseries

Status: Archived

Sampled data from deterministic non-linear systems may look stochastic when analysed with linear methods.

This project started as a course project in the Nonlinear Dynamics and Chaos course taught by Dr. Chandradew Sharma. The task was to understand and reproduce this paper, In the process, I implemented nonlinear models from scratch such as global polynomials, local polynomials, neural networks and semi-local methods including radial basis functions.

Taking this project further I wish, to build and compare a generative latent VAE-based chaotic timeseries model using LSTMs and Neural ODEs (employing different sequence bucketing techniques). I also wish to explore expressing timeseries in terms of wavelets and splines.
